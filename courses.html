Undergraduate Courses

These are the courses offered to undergraduate computer science students:

  • CS101 - Introduction to Computer Science
  • CS201 - Data Structures and Algorithms
  • CS301 - Operating Systems
  • CS401 - Database Management Systems
  • CS501 - Software Engineering

Graduate Courses

For graduate computer science students, the following courses are available:

  • CS601 - Advanced Algorithms
  • CS701 - Artificial Intelligence
  • CS801 - Machine Learning
  • CS901 - Distributed Systems
  • CS1001 - Cybersecurity

Research Opportunities

Computer science students have the opportunity to engage in various research projects and special topics. Some of the ongoing research areas include:

  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Computer Vision
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Quantum Computing
